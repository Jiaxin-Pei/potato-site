About Potato Annotator

The story behind our powerful annotation platform

Potato Annotator was founded by a team of researchers and engineers with a passion for making data annotation accessible to everyone. Our platform has evolved from a simple annotation tool to a comprehensive solution that powers research and AI development worldwide.

Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality annotated data and to help organizations make better decisions based on human insights. With a focus on simplicity, reliability, and ethical data practices, we're committed to supporting the advancement of AI and research for the benefit of humanity.
